cell\_count & Number of labeled adherent cells per field & High (>200--500/field) & Decreasing over days & Direct quantitative measure of cell detachment and loss of adhesion; classic early CPE hallmark in Vero monolayers. \\
confluency\_percent & Percentage of image area occupied by cells & >70--90\% & <40--60\% in advanced stages & Tracks overall monolayer disruption and coverage loss; highly sensitive to serum-stress or viral-induced detachment without staining. \\
mean\_area\_px & Average cell area in pixels & 800--2000 px & <600 px (rounding/shrinkage) & Reflects cell shrinkage or swelling; smaller area is a reliable morphological indicator of rounding and stress in adherent epithelial cultures. \\
mean\_circularity & Degree of cell roundness (4π·area/perimeter², 0--1) & 0.3--0.6 & 0.7--1.0 & Higher values directly quantify the transition from spread polygonal to rounded morphology, a core visual CPE feature. \\
mean\_eccentricity & Shape elongation descriptor (0 = circle, 1 = line) & 0.4--0.7 & 0.8--0.95 & Captures loss of elongation; increased eccentricity indicates rounding and is a robust, label-free proxy for cytopathic changes. \\
mean\_perimeter\_px & Average cell boundary length in pixels & Variable & Often lower in rounded cells & Reduced perimeter reflects boundary smoothing during rounding; complements circularity as an independent texture/morphology cue. \\
